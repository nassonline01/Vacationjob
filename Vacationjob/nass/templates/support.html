Rules & Guidelines

Task Submission Guidelines

Ensure you follow these guidelines when submitting your task proof:

  • Upload only accepted file formats (e.g., PDF, JPG).
  • Provide a detailed description of how you completed the task.
  • Make sure to submit before the task deadline.

Behavioral Guidelines

  • No fraudulent task submissions.
  • Respect other users and avoid inappropriate behavior.
  • Follow the platform's code of conduct at all times.

Consequences for Violations

Failure to follow these rules may result in:

  • Account suspension
  • Loss of task payouts
  • Permanent ban from the platform (for severe violations)

Payment Regulations

Payout Structure

Your payout is calculated based on the task payout and is processed once you submit your proof:

  • Payments will be processed on a weekly basis.
  • Minimum withdrawal amount: $50
  • Payments are sent via bank transfer, PayPal, etc.

Payment Methods

  • Bank Transfer
  • PayPal
  • Other methods (coming soon)

Tax Information

Users are responsible for reporting earnings as per their country's tax laws.

Payment Disputes

If you have any issues with your payment, please contact support immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I submit proof for a completed task?

Go to the task page, and click on the "Submit Proof" button. Upload your proof file and provide a description.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

Once your earnings reach the minimum withdrawal threshold, go to the "Withdraw" section in your profile, and choose a payment method.

How can I update my account details?

Visit the "Profile" page in your dashboard, and click on "Edit Profile" to update your information.
